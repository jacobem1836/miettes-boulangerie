Our story

From a simple loaf to an institution

From a simple sliced white to an artisan loaf — each hand crafted with love, time and tradition. Miettes Boulangerie Patisserie brings traditional European energy and standards to the age-old practice of making bread and pastries in the heart of Brisbane's inner west.

The bakery offers an extensive range of products including our renowned sourdoughs — Organic Whole-wheat, Heritage Sustainable White, Linseed and Sunflower, Spelt, Ancient Grain and Haloumi and Mint, to name a few. We also bake traditional baguettes, cornbread, Turkish bread and ciabatta alongside baskets of other freshly baked goods every morning.

Miettes also offers a selection of French-style patisserie, cakes and biscuits to indulge your taste buds. Think flaky croissants and pastries, delectable dessert cakes and perfection pies. Our bread is good enough that we supply various types of sourdough and Turkish bread to other restaurants in the neighbourhood.

Why people come back

Six good reasons to visit

Baked fresh daily

Everything is baked on site every morning, starting before 6am. The cabinet is fresh — and when things sell out, they are gone for the day.

Two convenient locations

Graceville on the river and Corinda along Oxley Road — both in Brisbane's inner west. Open six days a week from 6am.

Remarkable value

The apple crumble tart serves four people. Sandwiches from $11.30. Pies and quiches under $11. One of the best-value bakeries in Brisbane.

Artisan methods

Proper laminated pastry. Long-fermented sourdoughs. Deck oven baking. The kind of methods that most bakeries have abandoned because they take too long.

Community favourite

The queues outside on weekend mornings are the most honest form of review. Regulars come every week. Some have been coming for years.

Campos coffee

Campos coffee, brewed on a La Marzocco machine. The kind of cafe quality that earns its own mention in almost every customer review.

Two locations

Find us in the inner west

Both locations open six days a week from 6am. Plenty of parking at Graceville — and easy to reach by train or bus from either side of the city.
